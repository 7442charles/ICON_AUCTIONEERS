Full service auctioneering, on and off the yard

Beyond bidding, Icon Auctioneers handles the legal and logistical work behind every asset we list — for lenders, courts and private clients alike.

Repossession

Secure, fully documented recovery of financed vehicles, equipment and property on behalf of banks and lenders, carried out within the bounds of the law.

Court Order Execution

Enforcement of court warrants and orders under our Class B auctioneering license, handled with the discretion the process demands.

Public Auction

Open-bid sales run transparently, both on-site and online, for vehicles, machinery, office and household assets across every regional yard.

Investigations

Due-diligence and asset tracing ahead of a recovery or listing, so every auction we run stands on clear title and accurate records.

Debt Collection

Structured, lawful recovery processes for overdue commercial and personal debt, with auction as a last resort where required.
